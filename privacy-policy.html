Privacy Policy for Spam Filter Assistant

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Spam Filter Assistant is a Gmail add-on that helps users classify emails as spam or not spam.

Information We Access

When you click Analyze Current Email, the add-on reads only the currently opened Gmail message subject and plain text body.

How We Use Information

The selected email content is sent to our prediction API to classify the message as Spam or Not Spam. The result is returned to your Gmail sidebar.

Data Storage

We do not intentionally store full email content in a permanent database through the add-on workflow. Service providers may keep short-term logs for reliability and security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is processed only to provide spam classification functionality.

Security

All API communication is performed over HTTPS.

User Controls

You can stop using the add-on at any time by uninstalling it from your Google account.
